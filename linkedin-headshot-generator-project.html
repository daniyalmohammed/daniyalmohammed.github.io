LinkedIn Headshot Generator Description


LinkedIn Headshot Generator is a professional iOS app built with SwiftUI that transforms your selfies into LinkedIn-ready headshots using Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash Image AI through OpenRouter. Whether you're updating your professional profile or need a polished headshot for networking, this app delivers professional-quality results from the comfort of your phone. Our app combines cutting-edge AI technology with an intuitive user interface to create stunning professional headshots. Simply take a selfie or select from your photo library, choose your preferred style settings, and let our AI transform your image into a LinkedIn-worthy professional headshot. Perfect for job seekers, professionals, entrepreneurs, and anyone looking to enhance their online presence, LinkedIn Headshot Generator makes professional photography accessible and affordable.

Architecture

LinkedIn Headshot Generator is built with modern iOS development practices using SwiftUI for a native, responsive user interface. The app leverages Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash Image AI through OpenRouter's API to provide powerful headshot generation capabilities.

Key Technical Components:

  • SwiftUI Framework: Modern, declarative UI development for iOS
  • Google Gemini 2.5 Flash Image AI: Advanced image generation and enhancement
  • OpenRouter Integration: Secure API gateway for AI services
  • Core Image: Local image processing and manipulation
  • Photos Framework: Seamless integration with iOS photo library
  • Share Sheet: Native iOS sharing capabilities

The app follows Apple's Human Interface Guidelines and implements best practices for privacy, security, and user experience. All image processing is handled securely with clear data flow transparency and user control over their personal data.
