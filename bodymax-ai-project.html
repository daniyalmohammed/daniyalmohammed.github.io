BodyMax AI Description


BodyMax AI is a revolutionary fitness analysis app that uses advanced computer vision and machine learning to deliver detailed body composition assessments from a single photo. With BodyMax AI, you can measure your body fat percentage, muscle mass distribution, and potential transformation outcomes with remarkable accuracy. Our app empowers fitness enthusiasts, athletes, and individuals on their health journey with scientific insights previously available only through specialized equipment or professional assessments. Simply upload a photo, and our AI will analyze dozens of body parameters to provide a comprehensive breakdown of your physique. Track your progress over time with detailed historical data, allowing you to see exactly how your body is responding to your fitness regimen and nutrition plan. BodyMax AI also provides personalized recommendations based on your specific body composition and fitness goals, whether you're aiming to lose fat, build muscle, or achieve a more balanced physique. Share your transformation journey with friends or a personal trainer, with easy-to-understand visualizations and metrics that highlight your progress. BodyMax AI makes body composition tracking accessible, accurate, and actionable for everyone.

Information Collection and Use

Images and Analysis: The photos you upload for body analysis are processed primarily on your device whenever possible. When server processing is required, images are transmitted using encrypted connections, processed for analysis, and then promptly deleted from our servers after processing is complete. The analysis results and metrics derived from these images are stored in your user account.

User Account Information: We collect basic account information such as email address, username, and password to enable your account. You may optionally provide information like height, weight, age, and fitness goals to improve analysis accuracy.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data to improve our services, including how you interact with the app, features used, and performance metrics. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Local Storage and Data Control: Most of your data, including your body analysis history, is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time through the app settings.
