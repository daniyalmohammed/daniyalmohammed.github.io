About Me

Hi, my name is Daniyal Mohammed! I am a first-year student at the University of Waterloo who aims to be academically stimulated by the disciplines of math. I take pride in my pursuit for finding solutions and analyzing problems from a diversity of different perspectives.

My interests stem from the tools used to solve these problems; from analytical processes (data science, game theory and optimization) to critical theory (philosophy, human systems, strategic leadership).

I have pretty basic hobbies: basketball, podcasts and travelling. I strive to put myself outside of my comfort zone and try new things which is the purpose for this website. My hope is to refine my writing skills and challenge myself to complete a thought-provoking blog post each month. Anyways, enjoy your stay on this site and feel free to contact me if you are interested in connecting.
