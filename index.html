About Me

Hi, my name is Daniyal Mohammed! I am a third-year Data Science student at the University of Waterloo who aims to be academically stimulated by the disciplines of math and computer science. I take pride in my pursuit for finding solutions and analyzing problems from a diversity of different perspectives.

My interests stem from the tools used to solve these problems; from analytical processes (statistics, software development and optimization) to critical theory (philosophy/history, game theory, strategic leadership).

I have pretty basic hobbies: basketball, podcasts, reading and travelling. My hope for this website is to refine my writing skills and challenge myself to post thought-provoking blogs and summarize my personal projects. Anyways, enjoy your stay on this site and feel free to contact me if you are interested in connecting.
